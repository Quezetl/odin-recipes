Steamed Egg Recipe

Ingredients Needed:

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/3 Cup Fish Stock
  • 2 Scallions(sliced)
  • 1 Tbspn Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tspn Sesame Oil

Steps:

  1. Scamble eggs with stock and soy sauce
  2. Preheat stone bowl
  3. Coat bowl with sesame oil
  4. Add mixture to bowl and cook with lid off stirring until bottom sets
  5. Cover and cook for 5 minutes
  6. Remove lid, top with Scallions and serve
