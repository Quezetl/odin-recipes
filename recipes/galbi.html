Galib Recipe

Ingredients Needed:

  • 1 lb Short Rib
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic(minced)
  • 2 Scallions(sliced)
  • 1/4 Asian Pear
  • 1/4 Cup Honey
  • 1 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tbspn Sesame Oil
  • 1 Tbpn Sesame Seeds

Steps:

  1. Clean meat and trim excess fat
  2. Mine Garlic
  3. Add Pear, Sesame Seeds, and liquids to blender then blend smooth
  4. Massage blended liquid with the garlic, meat and scallions
  5. Let it rest 1 hour at least or optionally over night
  6. Grill to desired doneness and serve with extra scallions
